#  SDG 7 & 13: Access to Clean Energy Technology in Developing Nations

For the Digital Equity project, I would like to focus on access to clean energy technology in developing nations. Many countries worldwide struggle to secure adequate access to clean energy and advanced technologies. Additionally, I intend to explore how nations like the U.S. are managing the growing energy demand driven by emerging technologies such as AI. As AI continues to advance in the United States, data center construction has surged, resulting in a significant increase in energy consumption. I aim to investigate how companies, such as Gemini from Google, source their energy and whether they are adopting cleaner solutions. This topic resonates with me, as I have engaged in conversations about these issues with my family and friends multiple times. I believe it is crucial for me to learn how clean energy is sourced in developed countries and how developing nations are working towards similar solutions.
